Government Surveillance of Social Media

Government surveillance of social media and user data is on the rise worldwide, and over the past five years surveillance has doubled in countries like the US, India, the UK, Germany, and France. This visualization is meant to give insight into governmental access imposed on the top social media and data collecting sites Google and Facebook. By visualizing the data Facebook and Google have released in their transparency reports we show the worldwide decrease in online privacy by region as time progresses.

The four products included within the Facebook transparency dataset are Facebook, Instagram, Facebook Messenger, and WhatsApp. The Google transparency dataset includes governmental requests on all data gathering products released by Google Inc. Each dataset spans years 2013-2017. For more information on each dataset visit our sources listed below.
